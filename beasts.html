The Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures is the second largest department in theMinistry of Magic. It is divided into three divisions named after the three categorisations of magical creatures: Being, Beast, and Spirit. It also houses the liaison offices for Goblins and Centaurs. The department is located on the fourth level of the Ministry of Magic, and includes the following divisions:

Beast Division
The Beast Division is one of the three divisions under the control of the department. It deals with all duties and problems concerning those magical creatures classified as a "Beast" by the Ministry.

A classification is given by the Department for the Regulation and Control of Magical Creatures to all known Beasts, Beings and Spirits. These offer an at-a-glance guide to the perceived danger level of a creature. There are five categories.
